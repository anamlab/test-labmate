The air permeability tester represents a cutting-edge technological tool designed for swift and precise measurement of air permeability across diverse materials. Operating as a fully automated system, it is under the control of a microcomputer, ensuring efficiency and accuracy. This advanced instrument boasts a wide measurement range, spanning from 0 to 2500 ml/min, and accommodates pressure differences from 0.05 to 6 kPa. Flexibility is a key feature, allowing users to obtain results in their preferred units of measurement. Furthermore, the air permeability tester incorporates user-friendly components, such as an LCD screen for intuitive navigation and a micro-printer for swift and legible result documentation.
